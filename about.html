My Journey

Why I Do This Work

I grew up with very little, so I know firsthand what financial pressure can feel like. Much of what people truly need to understand about debt, money, and financial decision-making is never taught in school or in college.

Too many people are left to learn through stress, struggle, and costly mistakes. That journey is a big part of what drives my passion for this work — even after more than three decades.

"Most people I speak with are not irresponsible — they are hardworking people doing everything they can, making payments, trying to stay current, and still finding themselves stuck. I believe people deserve clarity, not judgment."

My goal is to help people better understand their situation and move forward with dignity, confidence, and a clearer plan. Whether you're drowning in credit card debt or just feeling stuck, I'm here to help you see the path forward.

What I Believe

The Values Behind Every Conversation

🙌

Patience First

Everyone's situation is different. I take the time to listen, understand, and explain — never rushing you into a decision you're not ready for.

🎓

Education Over Sales

I'd rather teach you something that changes your financial life than sell you a service. When you understand how money works, everything changes.

🤝

Dignity Always

Debt doesn't define you. You'll never feel judged in our conversations. My job is to help you see options you didn't know you had.
